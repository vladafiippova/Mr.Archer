About Me

Hello! My name is Vlada, and I am an M.S. in Geospatial Technology student passionate about geology. I have a B.S. in Geology from Florida State University. Currently, I am pursuing M.S. in Geospatial Technology at the University of Washington, Tacoma. People who know me and my life path usually wonder how I could move across the world without any help or friends. Really, how a girl with limited resources got a degree from Florida State University in two years? My answer is straightforward. It took two hard years of work and learning, sleepless nights, and no time to rest. In reality, the outcome is quite breathtaking. I have ongoing research in earth materials explaining the elasticity of amphiboles under pressure and temperature conditions. The Capstone project for MSGT Program covers geoprocessing tools for visualizing petroleum bearing fields.
